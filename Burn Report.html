Before You Burn

Residents planning a controlled or agricultural burn must notify the Fire Department before igniting any fire.

Who to Contact

Non-Emergency Line: (555) 987-6543

Office Hours: Monday–Friday, 8am–5pm

Contact Us

When to Call 911

  • If the fire becomes uncontrolled
  • If flames spread beyond your property
  • If smoke creates traffic or visibility hazards

Controlled Burn Guidelines

  • Check local weather conditions
  • Have water nearby
  • Never leave fire unattended
