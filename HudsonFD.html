ℹ️ About Us

The Hudson Fire Department is dedicated to protecting our community through emergency response, fire prevention, and public education. Our trained firefighters serve with honor, courage, and commitment. We serve the town of Hudson, South Dakota and aid the surrounding areas.


We operate as part of the South Dakota Firefighters Association, under Southeast District #1. We proudly serve our state and perform our sworn duties to respond to emergencies, protect people and property, and fight fires.

🎯 Mission Statement
