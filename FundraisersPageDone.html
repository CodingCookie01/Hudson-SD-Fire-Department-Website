How Your Donation Helps

Donating to the Hudson Fire Department helps support our mission to protect and serve the community by saving lives and preventing property damage through fire prevention and emergency medical services. Your contribution ensures that those in need are treated with compassion and integrity, with their care and protection as the highest priority.

These funds help provide essential equipment, training, and resources that allow first responders to act quickly and effectively in emergencies. By donating, you are directly investing in the safety and well-being of your community, because our community is our protection.

Donations can be made in person at the station or during community events.
